  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Family: Felidae
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Genus: Panthera

The lion (Panthera leo) is one of the big cats in the genus Panthera and a member of the family Felidae. The commonly used term African lion collectively denotes the several subspecies in Africa. With some males exceeding 250 kg (550 lb) in weight,[4] it is the second-largest living cat after the tiger.
